Auschwitz concentration camp
The camp was built in 1940 and lasted until 1945. During that time, more than 17,000 Austrians suffered there. It's a sad part of history that reminds us about the terrible things that happened during World War II.

Prisoner Gender

Prisoner Age
The age of the prisoners at the camp varied widely, spanning from a few months to 88 years. However, the majority fell within the age range of 30 to 60 years old.
